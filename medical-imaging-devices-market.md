<p><p>The medical imaging devices market comprises various types, each serving distinct diagnostic purposes. Computer Tomography (CT) provides detailed cross-sectional images using X-rays for internal examination. Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) utilizes magnetic fields and radio waves for detailed soft tissue imaging. Positron Emission Tomography (PET) detects metabolic processes, aiding in cancer diagnosis. Ultrasound employs sound waves for real-time imaging, particularly in obstetrics. X-Ray is a widely used method for examining bone structures and detecting abnormalities. Other imaging modalities encompass emerging technologies enhancing diagnostic capabilities.</p></p>
