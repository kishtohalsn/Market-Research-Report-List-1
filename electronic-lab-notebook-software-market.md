<p><p>Electronic Lab Notebook (ELN) software encompasses two primary market types: cloud-based and web-based solutions. Web-based ELNs, on the other hand, run directly in a web browser without requiring local installation, offering simplicity and ease of use. Both types enhance data management, provide secure storage, and streamline research processes, catering to the evolving needs of laboratories.</p></p>
