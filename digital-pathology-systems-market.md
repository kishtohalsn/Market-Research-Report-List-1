<p><strong>The Digital Pathology Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static Telepathology</li><li>Dynamic Telepathology</li><li>Hybrid Telepathology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital pathology systems encompass various types, including static telepathology, dynamic telepathology, and hybrid telepathology. Static telepathology involves capturing and sharing images of tissue samples for remote diagnosis, often with predefined protocols. Dynamic telepathology facilitates real-time, interactive consultations between pathologists, allowing immediate feedback and collaboration. Hybrid telepathology combines elements of both methods, enabling both static image sharing and live video interactions, thereby enhancing diagnostic accuracy and efficiency. Each type caters to different needs in remote pathology services and clinical workflows.</p></p>
